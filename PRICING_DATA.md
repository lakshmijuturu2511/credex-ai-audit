# Pricing Data

## Sources Referenced
- OpenAI ChatGPT Team and Enterprise pricing

## Assumptions
- Pricing estimates are based on publicly available monthly plans.
- Seat-based calculations were used for team pricing.
- Estimated savings are calculated using usage optimization heuristics.
